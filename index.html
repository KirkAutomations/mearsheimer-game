How to Play

🎯 Objective

Survive and maximize your power from 2000 to 2050. Control regions, build military and economic power, navigate crises. The AI plays by the same offensive realist rules you do.

🏆 Victory Conditions

  • Regional Hegemony — Control 9+ of 12 regions
  • Power Supremacy — End with the highest total power
  • Theory Mastery — Unlock all 19 Mearsheimer concepts

⚔ Each Turn (1 year)

  • 4 Action Points — spend on military, economy, intel, diplomacy, or force projection
  • Click a region on the map to select it, then deploy, attack, fortify, or spy
  • End Turn — AI acts, events fire, income collected, mini-games may trigger

💣 Mini-Games

  • Brinkmanship — Escalation chicken. Push tension or back down. Hit the threshold = war.
  • Fog of War — Intel assessment. Flip cards, separate real signals from deception.
  • Nuclear Alert — 15 seconds. Launch or ride it out? Is it a false alarm?
  • Proxy War — Resource allocation in a contested zone without direct confrontation.

💡 Key Mechanics

  • Water crossings reduce deployment size (stopping power of water)
  • Every military build makes other powers more hostile (security dilemma)
  • Buck-passing redirects threats but risks credibility
  • Nuclear arsenals deter direct war but cost heavily
  • Relative power matters — it's not about your total, it's about the gap
